Short description (70/80)

Transcribe and translate voice notes with your own OpenRouter API key.

Full description

SayCopy turns short voice recordings into text you can edit, copy, share, and keep in a local history. Record naturally, transcribe speech, or translate text into your chosen language. Optional light cleanup makes the result easier to reuse while preserving what you meant. You bring your own OpenRouter API key and choose from the models available in SayCopy. The app is free, but selected models may charge usage to your OpenRouter account. Designed for a clear, focused workflow: • Record, pause, resume, cancel, or finish when you are ready • Transcribe speech or translate recorded and typed text • Copy results or open the Android share sheet • Save, search, edit, tag, and delete history on your device • Keep preferences and history in local app storage • Store your OpenRouter key in secure device storage SayCopy has no SayCopy account, subscription, advertising, cross-app tracking, or analytics SDK. Audio and text leave your device only when you request processing. Those requests are sent to OpenRouter and eligible model providers under their policies and your OpenRouter account settings.

Reviewer instructions

SayCopy does not use a SayCopy account. Remote processing requires a temporary OpenRouter API key supplied for review. 1. Open Settings. 2. Paste the temporary review key in “OpenRouter token.” 3. Tap “Save token.” 4. Return to Record. 5. Tap the microphone, allow microphone permission, speak a short phrase, and stop. 6. The transcription appears and can be copied, shared, or opened from History. 7. To test translation, choose Translate, select source/target languages, and record or type a short phrase. The review key is limited and may be revoked after review. If it does not work, contact tarek@altitudeinfosys.com. Do not publish this key in the store listing.
