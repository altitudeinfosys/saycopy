About 2 minutes

Connect SayCopy to OpenRouter.

SayCopy does not require its own account. You provide an OpenRouter API key so requests are billed directly to your OpenRouter account, then choose the speech and text models you prefer.

Create an OpenRouter account Open API keys

Before you start

  • You need an OpenRouter account and a small credit balance.
  • Your key should be created for your use of SayCopy and kept private.
  • SayCopy shows models that can use zero-data-retention routing, but model prices and availability are controlled by OpenRouter.
1

Create or sign in to OpenRouter

Visit openrouter.ai and sign in. OpenRouter is a separate service with its own terms and privacy policy.

2

Add credits and set a limit

Add a small amount of credit, then set a spending limit that fits your use. SayCopy itself is free, but the models you choose may charge for usage through OpenRouter.

3

Create an API key

Open OpenRouter Keys, create a key for SayCopy, and copy it. A separate key makes it easy to monitor or revoke access later.

4

Save it in SayCopy

In the SayCopy app, open Settings, paste your key into the OpenRouter token field, and tap Save token. The key is stored in the platform's secure storage on your device.

5

Choose your preferred models

In Settings, use Browse transcription models for speech-to-text and Browse translation models for translation and cleanup. These are separate because the two jobs use different kinds of models. If you are unsure, tap Use recommended defaults.

Your preferred transcription model is used when you select English, Spanish, or Arabic. Auto-detect uses a separate GPT-4o Transcribe engine, so choosing Auto does not replace your preferred model.

6

Record, then copy or share

Return to Record, choose Transcribe or Translate, and speak. When the result is ready, copy it or share it into another app.

Why are there two model choices?

Transcription models listen to audio and return text. Translation and cleanup models receive that text and improve or translate it. A model may support one job without supporting the other, so SayCopy keeps the choices separate.

How does Auto-detect work?

Auto-detect uses GPT-4o Transcribe to identify the spoken language and preserve it in the transcript. Your preferred model stays saved and is used whenever you select a language explicitly.

What does Light cleanup do?

When enabled in Transcribe mode, the active text model improves punctuation, spacing, capitalization, and small artifacts without translating the transcript. Turning it off returns the raw speech-to-text result.

What does it cost?

SayCopy does not add a model subscription. OpenRouter charges your account according to the models you select and shows usage in its activity dashboard. Start with a small balance and spending limit until you understand your normal usage.

Troubleshooting

Auto-detect and my preferred model show different names

This is expected. Auto-detect has a dedicated engine, while the preferred model is used for recordings where you select English, Spanish, or Arabic. See the language and model guide for examples.

The token is not accepted

Copy the complete key again from OpenRouter, make sure no spaces were added, and confirm the key has not been disabled or limited to different models.

A selected model does not run

Return to Settings and select the model from the appropriate in-app OpenRouter list. Availability can change, and an account or key-level model restriction can also prevent routing.

A request says payment is required

Check your OpenRouter balance and key spending limit. SayCopy cannot add credits or change limits on your behalf.

Keep your key private

Treat an API key like a password. Do not post it, email it, or share screenshots that show it. If you think it has been exposed, revoke it in OpenRouter and create a new one.

Need help? Use the SayCopy contact form without including your API key.
